Welcome to Ziva's Dashboard
A quick walkthrough to get you started — 5 steps
1
Log meals with Quick Log Tap the + button at the bottom → Feed → pick a meal (B/L/D/S) → type what Ziva ate → Save. Takes 10 seconds.
2
Log sleep & poop Same + button → Sleep (bedtime + wake time) or Poop (consistency + color). Naps have their own option too.
3
The Ziva Score The big number on Home combines sleep, diet, poop, medical & milestones into one score. It drops when data isn't logged — not because Ziva is doing poorly, but because the app needs your input. Tap it to see the breakdown.
4
"Can I Give This?" In the Track → Diet tab, scroll to the Can I Give This? card. Type any food to check if it's safe for Ziva's age, what nutrients it has, and what to pair it with.
5
Add new foods to the tracker In Track → Diet, find the Foods Introduced card. Type the food name, set the date, and mark it OK or Watch. This builds Ziva's food history and powers the nutrition analysis.

iPhone: Tap the Share button (⬆) → "Add to Home Screen"

To sync data: Use Settings → Data → Share Backup. Send the file via WhatsApp or any app, then import on the other phone.
Your data lives in this browser's localStorage (~5 MB limit). Export regularly via the Data tab to keep a backup.
